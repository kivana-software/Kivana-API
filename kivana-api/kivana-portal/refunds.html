Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-12

This Refund Policy applies to all purchases and subscriptions for Kivana. By purchasing or subscribing to Kivana, you agree to this policy.
Free trial
Kivana offers a 14-day free trial for new users.
No payment is required during the trial period.
You may cancel at any time before the trial ends.
If you cancel before the trial expires, you will not be charged.
The trial is provided to allow evaluation of the product before any financial commitment.
Billing
Subscriptions begin only after the free trial ends.
Once the trial expires, your selected payment method will be charged automatically.
Billing cycles are disclosed at the time of purchase.
Refunds
After the trial ends and a subscription payment has been processed, all payments are non-refundable. This includes partial billing periods, unused time, and failure to cancel before renewal.
Right of withdrawal (EEA consumers)
If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), by starting a subscription after the trial and using the service, you explicitly request immediate access to digital content and acknowledge that you lose your right of withdrawal once the service has been fully delivered.
Exceptions
Refunds may be issued at our sole discretion in limited cases such as duplicate charges, technical issues preventing access, or billing errors. To request a review, contact support with full details.
Cancellations
You may cancel your subscription at any time.
Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
No further charges will be applied after cancellation.
No refunds will be issued for the current billing period.
Chargebacks
Initiating a chargeback without contacting support first may result in immediate account suspension and permanent restriction from future use. Contact support to resolve billing issues.
Changes to this policy
This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of Kivana after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
